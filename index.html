Getting Involved

How can I get involved with the AI Incubator? +
There are several ways to get involved with DO GOOD X's AI Incubator:
  • Apply as a Participant: Join our 8-week intensive program to build your AI-first business
  • Attend our Webinar: Learn more about the program and meet the facilitators
  • Become a Mentor: Share your expertise with our cohort of social entrepreneurs
  • Partner with Us: Help us expand our impact through strategic partnerships
  • Stay Informed: Sign up for updates about future programs and events
The best way to start is by attending our information webinar or reaching out to our team at hello@dogoodx.org.
What is this program? +
An 8-week intensive program that teaches founders, small business owners, and creatives how to build AI-first businesses using rapid validation, ethical development practices, and strategic frameworks proven in venture capital.

You'll take one idea from problem validation to functioning MVP with real customer traction, while learning the skills to become a serial founder in the AI era.
Who is this program for? +
Three primary archetypes:
  • Evening Entrepreneurs People with day jobs who want to build a business on the side with structure and accountability
  • Purpose-Driven Builders Social impact founders who want to leverage AI responsibly to 10x their mission
  • Small Business Owners/Creatives People with existing traction who want to use AI to scale without hiring huge teams
Who is this NOT for? +
  • People without a specific problem they're trying to solve
  • People who can't commit 3+ hours per week for 8 weeks
  • People who aren't in a financial position to invest $900
  • People looking to build hardware products requiring manufacturing
  • People who view AI ethics as optional or a hindrance

Logistics

When does the program run? +
  • Start Date: March 9, 2026
  • End Date: April 27, 2026
  • Duration: 8 weeks
  • Sessions: 2 per week, 90 minutes each (16 total sessions)
  • Time: March to April 2026, Evenings
Is this in-person or virtual? +
100% virtual. All sessions are conducted via Zoom.
What if I miss a session? +
All sessions are recorded and you'll have access to recordings. However, the real value is in attending live for real-time feedback, collaboration, and network building.

Missing 1-2 sessions due to travel or emergencies is understandable. Missing more than that means you won't get the full value of the cohort experience.
Can I join from outside the US? +
The program is virtual, so technically yes. However, sessions will be scheduled for the Eastern Time Zone. If you're in a drastically different timezone where sessions would be at 3 AM, consider whether that's sustainable for 8 weeks.
What's the cohort size? +
Maximum: 15 participants (to maintain quality and cohort dynamics)

Curriculum & Content

What will I actually learn? +
  • Week 1 AI-powered market intelligence and competitive analysis
  • Week 2 Rapid MVP development and ethical AI integration
  • Week 3 Beta testing and market validation frameworks
  • Week 4 AI-first organizational leadership
  • Week 5 Business model innovation and financial strategy
  • Week 6 Strategic network architecture and cap table design
  • Week 7 Data-driven growth and scale strategy
  • Week 8 Strategic launch and market positioning
  • Capstone Showcase with investors and strategic partners
What's the teaching methodology? +
  • Problem-first, tool-second approach: Focus on solving real problems, not just learning tools
  • Experimental framework: You're a scientist running tests, not an entrepreneur guessing
  • Hands-on application: You build and test your actual business throughout the program
  • Cohort-based learning: Learn from peers, give and receive feedback, build your network
  • Expert facilitation: Direct access to Keyona's VC experience and global insights
What AI tools will we learn? +
We focus on principles and frameworks that work across tools, not specific tool training. However, you'll be exposed to:
  • SLMs & LLMs (like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini)
  • No-code development platforms (Lovable, Bolt, Cursor)
  • Data analysis and market research tools
  • Automation and workflow tools
  • Local and open-source model options
The specific tools matter less than understanding when and how to use different categories of tools.
Do I need to pay for AI tool subscriptions? +
No. AI tool stipends are included in your tuition. You'll receive credits or access to necessary tools.
Will there be homework? +
Yes. Each week you'll have assignments:
  • Customer interviews
  • Building/testing your MVP
  • Market research exercises
  • Reading/preparation for next session
  • Peer feedback on cohort members' work
Budget approximately 3-5 hours per week outside of the 90-minute sessions.
